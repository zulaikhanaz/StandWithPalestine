Our Mission

To educate, empower, and mobilize individuals and communities to support Palestinian rights and human dignity. We aim to turn awareness into action through campaigns, events, and accessible resources that make solidarity tangible and impactful.

Our Vision

A world where Palestinians live free from oppression, enjoy equal rights, and can determine their own future. We envision a society built on justice, compassion, and international solidarity.

Our History

Founded by passionate students and activists, Stand With Palestine started as a grassroots initiative to inform and engage local communities. Over the years, we have grown into a global movement, connecting voices, amplifying campaigns, and contributing to international advocacy for justice and peace.
